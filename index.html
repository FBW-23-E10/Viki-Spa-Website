Saunas

The spacious sauna area of ​​the Lotus spa extends over two floors and the outdoor area. A total of 7 exceptional and relaxing saunas are available to guests: panoramic sauna, bio sauna, garden sauna, meditation sauna, several large infusion saunas, two steam baths and a laconium.

The abundance of infusions ranges from classic salt, honey and fruit infusions to invigorating meditation and herbal treatments.

Facials

Treat your skin to what it deserves!

In the Meridian Face & Body Day Spa you will experience tailor-made beauty concepts - tailored to your needs. A caring pleasure for the youthfulness of your skin. Effective, extraordinary and simply beautiful.

SUPER CLEAN

The Super Clean Facial, with its medical-cosmetic active ingredients, works effectively against impure skin caused by hormones, unhealthy lifestyle habits and predisposition. After a detailed anamnesis and cleansing of the skin, dead skin cells are removed with an enzymatic peeling.

25 Min. - 29 €

DEEP PORE CLEANSING FACIAL

Great for all skin types, this customized facial consists of a custom treatment mask, extractions and a variety of cleansers that reach deep down into the pores to absorb and remove dirt, oil and toxins. The use of exfoliators further complements the treatment by removing dead skin cells.

25 Min. - 32 €

HYDRATING FACIAL

This facial is for those with dry skin. It includes the use of hydrating cleansers before exfoliation and extractions are performed. Then, a hydrating mask and rich moisturizers are applied that will keep the skin hydrated long after the treatment is over.

25 Min. - 29 €

EYE TREATMENT

Accompanying any facial, this treatment is designed to minimize fine lines, hydrate and brighten the eye area while reducing puffiness.

25 Min. - 15 €

FACIAL MASSAGE

An effective facial massage that was developed on the basis of massage and physiotherapy. The result is an immediate lifting effect with clear facial features and smoother contours.

25 Min. - 25 €

MICRODERMABRASION

This treatment can be performed along with your chosen or customized facial. Microdermabrasion involves the use of small abrasive crystals and a suctioning action, to lightly strip away the outer layer of skin, leaving it polished and smooth.

25 Min. - 40 €
